🪳 PEOPLE'S MANIFESTO 2025

COCKROACH JANATA PARTY MANIFESTO

For decades citizens were asked to trust politicians. We believe citizens should trust data instead. The Cockroach Janata Party exists to expose problems, compare states, track accountability and encourage evidence-based governance.

🏛 Governance

Transparent Spending

Every public project should have a public dashboard showing budgets, deadlines and progress.

Citizen Accountability

Citizens should rate public services continuously, not just politicians every five years.

Performance Rankings

States should compete using measurable outcomes, not speeches and slogans.



🏥 Healthcare

Hospital Transparency

Track hospital quality, doctor availability and emergency response times.

Medicine Access

Essential medicines should be available and monitored publicly.

Healthcare Rankings

Compare states using real health outcomes.



🎓 Education

School Scorecards

Attendance, infrastructure and results should be transparent.

Teacher Accountability

Every child deserves quality education and measurable standards.

Future Skills

Focus on technology, science and entrepreneurship.



💼 Economy

Jobs First

Track employment generation across states.

Support Innovation

Encourage startups and small businesses.

Data Driven Policy

Decisions should be based on evidence, not emotion.

🔥 The Cockroach Promise

No impossible promises. No magic solutions. No fake statistics.

We promise transparency. We promise accountability. We promise uncomfortable facts.

"If a state performs better, citizens deserve to know. If a government fails, citizens deserve to know."
