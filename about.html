🪳 ABOUT THE MOVEMENT

Who Are We?

Cockroach Janata Party (CJP) is not a traditional political party. It is a citizen-powered satirical movement created to expose problems, compare states, encourage accountability and make governance discussions more engaging for ordinary people.

🎯 Our Mission

To make public data easy to understand. Citizens deserve to know which states perform better, which governments deliver results and where improvement is needed.

📊 Data Over Drama

Most politics is driven by speeches and emotions. CJP believes decisions should be based on measurable outcomes, rankings and transparent public information.

🏆 Healthy Competition

When states compete for better education, healthcare, infrastructure and jobs, citizens become the real winners.

🪳 Why A Cockroach?

Because cockroaches survive everything. Governments change. Parties change. Promises change. Problems remain. The cockroach reminds us that citizens outlast politicians.

"India does not need more political slogans. India needs transparency, accountability and informed citizens."
