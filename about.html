About Cockroach Janata Party (CJP)

Cockroach Janata Party (CJP) is a satirical citizen-driven platform created to discuss India's challenges and explore practical solutions.

Our mission is to identify problems across Indian states and union territories and encourage constructive public discussion through data, ratings, and community participation.

CJP is not affiliated with any political party, government organization, or election commission. This website is intended for educational, informational, and satirical purposes only.

We believe every citizen deserves transparency, accountability, and better governance.
