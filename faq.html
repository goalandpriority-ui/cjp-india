❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions Citizens Ask

Everything you wanted to know about the world's first insect-powered political movement.

🪳 Is Cockroach Janata Party a real political party?

No. CJP is a satirical citizen project designed to discuss governance, state performance, public accountability and citizen participation.

📊 Are the rankings real?

Some rankings are based on public information, while others may be demonstration or experimental rankings. Always verify important information independently.

🏆 Why compare states?

Healthy competition encourages governments to improve education, healthcare, infrastructure, employment and public services.

🪳 Why a cockroach?

Because cockroaches survive everything. Governments come and go. Citizens remain. The cockroach symbolizes resilience.

🗳 Can I vote here?

You cannot vote for governments here, but you can participate in rankings, feedback systems and citizen discussions.

🚨 How can I contribute?

Share data, report issues, suggest improvements, submit feedback and help make public information easier to understand.

💰 Does CJP take donations?

Currently no. The project focuses on awareness, data visualization and public engagement.

🏛 Is CJP affiliated with any party?

No. CJP is independent and not connected to any political party, government institution or election authority.
