The Platform is provided for general informational, educational and civic engagement purposes. Nothing on the Platform constitutes legal, medical, financial or any other form of professional advice.

5. Citizen Ratings

The Rate Your State feature allows citizens to submit anonymous ratings of their state governments. By submitting a rating, you represent that:

  • Your rating reflects your genuine personal assessment of government service delivery in your state
  • You are a resident of or have direct experience with the state you are rating

CJP reserves the right to exclude or remove ratings that appear to be submitted in bad faith, as part of organised manipulation, or that otherwise do not reflect genuine citizen experience. CJP's determination of what constitutes a bad-faith rating is final.
